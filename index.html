Capstone Project Cooperated with Bloomberg HK

In this one-year capstone project, my team developed a tool for optimizing employee task schedule under stringent deadlines and constraints. We also performed time-series forecasting with Holt winter's method with mean absolute percentage error 8%. The final product was a personal schedule for every employee with a user interface. This project won the Outstanding Capstone Project Award in HKUST 2019. More information

Sentiment Analysis on Yelp Data

The project is to predict the rating (1-5) that a user will give to a restaurant based on the user’s text review on Yelp.

I implemented a deep learning algorithm (called DPCNN architecture) from a recent research paper and obtained an accuracy rate in our application of almost 70%. More information

Predict Credit Default Likelihood

In the course project, I evaluated credit risks based on clients’ payment history with logistic regression. More information
